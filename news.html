More Pain at the Pump yet again!!

The Energy and Petroleum Regulatory Authority (EPRA) has released the latest fuel price adjustments, effective from 14th July 2025 to 14th August 2025. As part of its monthly review, EPRA cited changes in global crude oil prices and forex fluctuations... Read More

New Pump Prices (Nairobi):
Petrol: Ksh 193.84 per litre
Diesel: Ksh 180.38 per litre
Kerosene: Ksh 170.06 per litre

These prices reflect a slight increase in petrol and diesel, while kerosene has remained relatively stable. The review is in line with EPRA's mandate to stabilize the petroleum market and protect consumers from volatile global energy prices.

What This Means:
Motorists and transport operators should brace for slightly higher operating costs this month. The ripple effect may also influence prices of goods and services, especially in the logistics and public transport sectors.

As always, EPRA encourages energy efficiency and prudent fuel usage during this period. Close Story
